Exploring Exoplanet and Habitability

One of the most captivating questions, in my view, is whether we are alone in the universe. As an astronomer, I contribute to answering this question by helping to identify the most promising observational targets in the search for life beyond Earth. This work relies largely on extrapolating what we know about life on our own planet to distant exoplanets. While life elsewhere is likely to differ greatly from life on Earth, our search must begin with the knowledge we have.
