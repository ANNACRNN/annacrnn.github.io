About

I am a postdoctoral researcher at CIERA, Northwestern University, studying the formation and evolution of binary stars and terrestrial planets. My research combines photometric data analysis, numerical simulations, and AI-based methodologies to better understand planetary systems and their dynamical histories.

Research Interests

  • Binary star formation and dynamics
  • Terrestrial planet formation, especially in M-dwarf and binary systems
  • Photometric analysis and modeling with Gaia DR3 data
  • AI and machine learning applications in astrophysics

Software & Tools

  • BASE9_utils: Pipeline for identifying photometric binaries using Gaia DR3 – GitHub
  • REBOUND extensions: Developed tools for fragmentation, composition tracking, and migration – GitHub
  • ZENITH: AI agent that summarizes and discusses astronomy papers in the style of Carl Sagan – @AgentzenithAi
